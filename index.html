When you played the game, how did you first approach it? Were you like most people, who just guessed numbers at random? When most people play this game, they just guess at random. If you guess at random, then sometimes you might get it right on the first try, but other times it might take you 10 or 20 tries, if not more, to get it right. You start at the middle of your possible range. In this case, since we're guessing numbers from 0 to 100, you should first start at 50. Assuming that the missing number isn't 50, you immediately cut out half of your possible numbers.
